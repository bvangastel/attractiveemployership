Analyzing Attractive Employership & Key Challenges

Goal

Please use the infographic Infographic – Attractive Employership, focusing on the three domains:

  • Professional Learning
  • Job Content / Task Design
  • Career Opportunities

Step 1 — Individual Reflection (15 min.)

Answer the questions below. Write your answers on post-its.

  1. What aspects of attractive employership from the infographic do you recognise in your school?
    (Write down concrete examples.)
  2. What important element from the infographic is missing or hardly visible in your school?
    (Identify one missed opportunity.)

Step 2 — Group Discussion (15 min.)

Discuss the collected post-its together in small groups:

  1. What is the biggest challenge in your school related to Attractive Employership?
    (e.g., onboarding, using teacher strengths, task distribution, learning culture, career pathways.)
  2. Why is this particularly difficult in your context?
    (e.g., time, culture, unclear roles, lack of policy, limited support.)
  3. Who is most affected by this challenge?
    (e.g., novice teachers, subject shortage areas, senior teachers, new hires, lateral entrants.)
  4. What has already been tried — and why has it not (yet) worked sufficiently?
